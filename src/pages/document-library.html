The report is included with “The Home Reference Book” in all services except for follow up inspections. This book is an encyclopedia on homes and will educate the owners on all maintenance needs, how various components of the home function, how to troubleshoot problems that arise and an estimated cost to repair/upgrade most areas of the home.

The reporting system used by Ridge Valley Home Inspections is “The Home Reference Book”. This encyclopedia of homes includes the report for the home at time of inspection, a home maintenance schedule, life cycles and costs to repair all areas of the home and a filing system for budgeting.
